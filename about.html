About Me

Some Context

I'm an Software Engineering student at the University of Calgary with a passion for creating. I started at the program in 2019, but fell in love with programming back in high school. Since then I've worked on a few personal projects with my friends, as well as projects for the classes I've taken. Although university and my studies have taken up much of my time for working on projects, I've always strived to create something that at least relates to something I've learned.
